Speaker Bio

People often ask for one of these. Here ya go.

Vignesh is a graduate student at New York University. He's worked for Radiator Labs, Verizon Communication, Sate Bank of India and Praudyogiki Technolabs, and did a bachelor's degree in computer science from Anna University in 2013. He's had the honor of working with various organizations, including Hacker Fund and VDSI. He's active developer in the computer society of India and National Entrepreneurship Network.

People who know vignesh best say that he's a go-getter person. He wants to inspire generations of STEM students to be the best they can be, and his favorite quote is from Helen Keller: "One can never consent to creep when one feels an impulse to soar". He also likes tacos and pop music.
